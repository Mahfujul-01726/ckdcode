🎯 TOP 7 FEATURES (XGBoost Selection)

   2. Serum Creatinine  - Kidney filtration marker
   3. PCV               - Red blood cell concentration
   4. Albumin           - Protein leakage indicator
   5. Pedal Edema       - Fluid retention sign
   6. Blood Pressure    - Hypertension comorbidity
   7. Specific Gravity  - Urine concentration ability

   All features are established CKD markers in clinical practice!
